Why We Do What We Do

About Us

At Petocracy, we absolutely love animals — and we think humans are pretty swell, too. That's why we created this site to help people just like you. Now more than ever, we understand that times can be tough especially with the pandemic.

We're motivated to help you

  • we're committed in creating a community where everyone and their pets are valued and respected.
  • we demonstrated our commitment in making a difference
  • at the heart of our collaborative spirit are our volunteers
  • every life is worth saving, each individual is important

Even today, it still comes down to small and simple acts of kindness. Every animal deserves a chance.

“I believe the happiness we feel in relation to animals is immensely significant. I believe it is absolutely central to our physical, psychological, and spiritual well-being.”

Linda Bender, Animal Wisdom: Learning from the Spiritual Lives of Animals
